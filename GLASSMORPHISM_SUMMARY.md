## KEY FEATURES IMPLEMENTED

### 🔮 Glass Material System
- **Background**: `rgba(20, 20, 28, 0.35)` with 24px blur
- **Saturation**: 180% for rich colors
- **Border**: Multi-layer with inner highlight
- **Depth**: 4-layer shadow system with red glow

### 🎭 3D Tilt Effect
- **Perspective**: 1000px with preserve-3d
- **Tilt Range**: ±7.5° on both axes
- **Duration**: 450ms easeOutExpo
- **Elevation**: Lifts 10px with 1.02 scale

### 💡 Cursor-Following Effects
- **Radial Light**: 200px circle follows cursor
- **Ambient Glow**: Extends beyond card boundaries
- **Dynamic**: Real-time tracking with smooth transitions

### ✨ Specular Reflection
- **Light Sweep**: 10s infinite loop
- **Angle**: 25° rotation
- **Opacity**: 8% white highlight
- **Movement**: Smooth left-to-right sweep

### 🌊 Holographic Top Panel
- **Glass Container**: Blurred red gradient
- **Moving Gradient**: 6s animation inside panel
- **Border**: Semi-transparent separator
- **Particles**: Floating ambient elements

### 🏷️ Glass Components
- **Live Badge**: Glass pill with red glow and pulsing dot
- **Skill Tags**: Glass capsules, fill red on hover
- **Buttons**: Glass with red gradient and shimmer
- **Progress Ring**: Enhanced with red glow

### 🎯 Micro-Interactions
- **Buttons**: Scale 1.05, icon rotate 5°, glow animation
- **Tags**: Lift -2px, scale 1.05, color transition
- **Corner Accents**: Fade in with red glow
- **Border Sweep**: 3s animated light around edges
